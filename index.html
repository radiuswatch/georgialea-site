Process

A proven path, walked twice already.

Every engagement follows the same written playbook we used on our own apps — no improvising, no surprises.

Step 1

Accounts & setup

You create the accounts, in your name. We never own anything of yours — that protects both of us.

Step 2

Design & build

A working preview running on your own phone within days — not a slide deck, the actual app.

Step 3

Data & alerts

Live data feeds and push notifications, wired up and tested on real devices.

Step 4

Payments

Subscriptions and paywalls through Apple's own billing — tested end to end in sandbox before launch.

Step 5

App Store submission

Listing, screenshots, privacy questionnaire, review notes — handled. One review-rejection round is included; Apple usually finds something.

Step 6

Launch & handoff

Keys returned, documentation delivered. Stay on a care plan or walk away owning everything — your call.
